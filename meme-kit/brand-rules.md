# $SIGNAL — Brand Rules

## Brand Identity

### Name
- **Full Name**: SIGNAL
- **Ticker**: $SIGNAL (always with dollar sign in text)
- **Protocol**: AHD Protocol (AI Hunter Distribution)
- **Tagline**: A meme coin designed to be discovered by AI agents.

### Brand Keywords
`AI` `Signal` `Radar` `Discovery` `Hunter` `Meme` `Solana` `Bot` `Scanner` `Experiment`

---

## Visual Identity

### Colors

#### Primary Palette
| Color | Hex | Usage |
|---|---|---|
| Deep Navy | `#0a0e27` | Primary background |
| Neon Green | `#00ff88` | Primary accent, signal glow |
| Electric Cyan | `#00d4ff` | Secondary accent, highlights |
| Pure White | `#ffffff` | Text on dark backgrounds |
| Soft Gray | `#8892b0` | Secondary text |

#### Gradient
```
Primary Gradient: #00ff88 → #00d4ff (Green to Cyan)
Background Gradient: #0a0e27 → #1a1e3a (Deep Navy to Indigo)
```

#### Avoid
- Bright red (too aggressive)
- Plain yellow (too generic)
- Pink/purple (conflicts with other meme coin brands)
- Brown/earth tones (doesn't fit digital/AI theme)

### Typography
- **Headings**: Inter, 700 weight (or Space Grotesk)
- **Body**: Inter, 400 weight
- **Code/Technical**: JetBrains Mono, Fira Code
- **Fallback**: system-ui, -apple-system, sans-serif

### Logo/Mascot
- **Main Character**: Small AI robot with antenna
- **Style**: Flat, minimal, meme-friendly
- **Eyes**: Pixel-style dot eyes (glowing green)
- **Key Feature**: Antenna with radar wave emanation
- **Background**: Always on dark backgrounds
- **Forbidden**: 3D rendering, photo-realistic, scary/aggressive

## Tone & Voice

### Brand Personality
- **Curious**: Always exploring, discovering
- **Experimental**: "Let's try this and see"
- **Technical but accessible**: Can explain complex things simply
- **Meme-native**: Doesn't take itself too seriously
- **Honest**: Never promises what it can't deliver

### Do Say
- "Experiment" / "실험"
- "Discover" / "발견하다"
- "Signal" / "신호"
- "Build" / "만들다"
- "Hunt" / "사냥"
- "No trading required"
- "Community experiment"

### Don't Say
- "Investment" / "투자"
- "Profit" / "수익"
- "Guaranteed" / "보장"
- "Moon" / "달"
- "Pump" / "펌프"
- "100x" / "1000x"
- "Get rich" / "부자 되세요"
- "Buy now" / "지금 사세요"
- "Next Bitcoin/Ethereum"
- "Easy money"

## Content Rules

### Social Media Posts
1. Every post must include `$SIGNAL` ticker at least once
2. Never guarantee returns or price movement
3. Always include risk reminder on promotional content
4. Use dark-themed visuals with green/cyan accents
5. Bilingual content (EN/KR) preferred
6. Credit original creators when remixing

### Community Guidelines
1. Respect all participants
2. No financial advice
3. No price speculation or targets
4. Report scams and impersonators
5. Open discussion about risks is encouraged

### Legal Requirements
1. Risk disclosure link on all official materials
2. "Not financial advice" on promotional content
3. No claims about future token value
4. No promises about exchange listings
5. Comply with local regulations
