## Do's and Don'ts

### ✅ Do
- Use the mascot robot character
- Reference AI discovery
- Keep it fun and experimental
- Use dark backgrounds with green/cyan accents
- Credit other creators
- Translate to multiple languages

### ❌ Don't
- Promise price increases
- Use rocket/moon imagery
- Say "guaranteed returns"
- Use "pump" excessively
- Copy other projects' mascots
- Make it look like a security offering
